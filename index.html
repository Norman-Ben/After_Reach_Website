Raw, powerful and emotionally-fuelled. Hailing from London UK, After Reach deliver a strong, uncensored narrative at the heart of their music, creating a destructive soundscape which attacks at an unrelenting pace. Setting up a strong foundation in 2021 with their debut single “Afraid of the Wind”, After Reach are ready to continue making noise to fully realise their musical vision with no compromises.
